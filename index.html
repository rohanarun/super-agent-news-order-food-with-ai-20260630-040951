How ordering food with an AI agent actually works

1. You state the intent

For example: “Order my usual Thai dinner” or “Get groceries for the week under my normal budget.”

The agent opens real delivery or grocery websites and apps, navigates menus, and fills the cart like a human would.

4. The computer-use cache kicks in

Future orders reuse learned flows, logins, and preferences from the computer-use cache instead of starting from scratch.
